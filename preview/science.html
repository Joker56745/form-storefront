Our standards

Science first.
No exceptions.

Every FORM formula starts with a clinical question, not a marketing brief. We pick the bioavailable form, dose to evidence thresholds, and publish the full supplement facts — no proprietary blends, no hidden fillers.

01

Bioavailable forms only

Magnesium glycinate over oxide. Methylcobalamin over cyanocobalamin. Cholecalciferol over ergocalciferol. The research-backed form, always.

02

Clinical doses

We dose to the levels used in peer-reviewed trials — not token amounts that make the label look full but do nothing physiologically.

03

Transparent labels

Full supplement facts on every product. No proprietary blends. No "matrix" language hiding underdosed ingredients.

04

No unnecessary additives

No titanium dioxide, no artificial dyes, no magnesium stearate in critical products. Excipients only where functionally necessary.

05

Third-party verified

Every batch is tested by an independent ISO-certified laboratory for identity, potency, and heavy metal contamination before it ships.

06

COA on request

Certificate of analysis for every production batch. Email us a batch number — we respond within 24 hours.

Testing protocol

From raw material to your door

  1. 1

    Ingredient sourcing audit

    Suppliers must provide third-party CoA, GMP certification, and origin documentation before we place an order.

  2. 2

    Incoming material testing

    Every raw ingredient is re-tested by our contract lab for identity and potency on arrival — regardless of supplier CoA.

  3. 3

    In-process QC

    Blend uniformity and weight variation checked at regular intervals throughout production.

  4. 4

    Finished product panel

    Potency, dissolution, heavy metals (Pb, Cd, As, Hg), and microbial limits on every finished batch.

  5. 5

    Release & archiving

    Batch released only after lab sign-off. CoA archived and available to any customer on request.
